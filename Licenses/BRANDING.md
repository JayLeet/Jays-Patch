# Jay's Patch Branding

The Jay's Patch name, logo, and visual branding are reserved by Jay.

You may:

- say that your server, fork, or add-on is compatible with Jay's Patch;
- say that your work is based on Jay's Patch;
- credit Jay's Patch in documentation, videos, server pages, or modpack notes.

You may not:

- present a modified version as the official Jay's Patch;
- claim that Jay made or approved your fork unless Jay actually did;
- remove Jay attribution and present the original Jay's Patch work as your own.

Forks and modified versions should use their own name and branding.
